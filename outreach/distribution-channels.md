# Distribution Channels for Comply

Research date: April 1, 2026. Focused on credit union compliance documentation tooling.

There are approximately 4,370 federally insured credit unions in the US (NCUA, mid-2025), over 1,000 registered CUSOs, and every state has its own credit union league. The compliance function is universal across all of them, which means the total addressable audience is large but fragmented across association memberships, state leagues, and vendor ecosystems.

---

## CUSOs (Reseller Partners)

CUSOs are the most natural distribution channel. They already sell compliance services to credit unions, and a documentation tool fits directly into their existing offering.

### NCUA CUSO Registry
- **URL:** https://ncua.gov/regulation-supervision/regulatory-reporting/cuso-registry
- **Type:** Official federal registry
- **Audience:** 1,012+ registered CUSOs (2021 figure; likely higher now)
- **Access:** Public data available (name, contact info, general services). Use this to build a prospecting list of CUSOs that list compliance as a service category.
- **Action:** Download the registry data, filter for compliance-related CUSOs, and cold-email with a partnership pitch.

### NACUSO (National Association of Credit Union Service Organizations)
- **URL:** https://www.nacuso.org
- **Type:** CUSO trade association
- **Audience:** 301+ members (CUSOs and credit unions)
- **Access:** Member directory is behind a login wall. Membership required for full access. They host an annual conference and networking events.
- **Action:** Apply for associate/vendor membership. Attend their conference. Their members are exactly the organizations that would resell a compliance tool.

### Comply-YES!
- **URL:** https://www.comply-yes.com
- **Type:** Compliance-focused CUSO
- **Audience:** 35+ credit union clients across 14 states
- **Access:** Direct outreach. They explicitly offer compliance outsourcing, auditing, and consulting.
- **Action:** Partnership pitch. They save CUs "up to 60% on the cost of a traditional FTE" for compliance. A documentation tool amplifies that value prop. Contact directly as a potential technology partner.

### CU*Answers
- **URL:** https://www.cuanswers.com
- **Type:** Technology CUSO (core processing + compliance)
- **Audience:** Cooperative of credit unions; hosts annual "Conversations on Compliance" event (11th annual in 2026)
- **Access:** Vendor partnership program available (see their "Vendor's Guide to CU*Answers" page).
- **Action:** Apply as a vendor partner. Pitch at their Conversations on Compliance event or CUSO Technology User Group.

### Curql Fund / Curql Accelerate
- **URL:** https://curql.com
- **Type:** Credit union fintech investment fund + accelerator
- **Audience:** Credit union investors and fintech startups
- **Access:** Accelerator invests $100K per company, 5 per cohort, 12-week program. Startups must become CUSOs as part of the investment.
- **Action:** Apply to Curql Accelerate (powered by gener8tor). This is the most structured path to credit union distribution, but requires incorporating as a CUSO.

## State Credit Union Leagues

Every state has a credit union league/association. These are the primary advocacy and support organizations for credit unions at the state level. Most offer compliance resources, training, and vendor partnerships to their members.

### AACUL League Locator (Master Directory)
- **URL:** https://aacul.com/league-locator
- **Type:** Directory of all 50 state leagues + DC
- **Audience:** Every credit union in the US is affiliated with a state league
- **Access:** Interactive map linking to each state league's website. Use this as the master list.
- **Action:** Identify the top 10 states by credit union count and contact their compliance departments.

- **URL:** https://www.infosight360.com
- **Type:** Compliance resource platform used by all 50 state leagues
- **Audience:** All state league member credit unions

- **Action:** Pitch an integration or content partnership. If Comply can feed into or complement InfoSight360, that is instant distribution to every league-affiliated CU.

## Online Communities

### America's Credit Unions Compliance Community
- **URL:** https://community.americascreditunions.org (requires membership)
- **Type:** Private online forum
- **Audience:** Compliance officers at member credit unions. America's Credit Unions represents the majority of US credit unions.
- **Access:** Membership benefit. Members can post, discuss, and collaborate. Direct access to the Compliance Team for federal compliance questions (response within one business day).
- **Action:** Become a vendor member of America's Credit Unions. Participate in the community. Do not hard-sell; contribute useful compliance documentation insights and build credibility.

### America's Credit Unions Compliance & Risk Council
- **URL:** https://www.americascreditunions.org/councils/membership/membership-benefits/compliance-risk-council-benefits
- **Type:** Professional council within America's Credit Unions
- **Audience:** Credit union staff with compliance or risk responsibilities. 400+ volunteers across all councils.
- **Access:** Membership requires holding a compliance/risk position at an affiliated CU. Vendor participation possible through sponsorship.
- **Action:** Sponsor council events or conference sessions.

### CCUA Compliance Network
- **URL:** https://www.ccua.org/compliance
- **Type:** Regional compliance network (Northeast US)
- **Audience:** Compliance staff at CCUA member credit unions
- **Access:** Regular meetings. Compliance hotline available (800-842-1242, option 4; complianceconnection@ccua.org).
- **Action:** Offer to present at a Compliance Network meeting. Frame as educational, not a sales pitch.

### The League Compliance Network
- **URL:** https://the-league.coop/communities
- **Type:** Regional compliance network (Midwest US)
- **Audience:** Compliance staff at League member credit unions
- **Access:** Regular meetings for sharing best practices, discussing common issues, and building industry relationships.
- **Action:** Same as CCUA: offer to present on documentation automation as an educational topic.

### Reddit
- **r/creditunions** — Small niche community. Mostly member-facing (consumers asking about CU services), not industry professionals. Low ROI for B2B tool distribution.
- **r/compliance** — General compliance community, not CU-specific. Occasional relevant threads.
- **r/personalfinance** — 17M+ members but consumer-focused, not industry.
- **Action:** Low priority. Monitor for relevant threads but do not invest time here for distribution.

### LinkedIn Groups
- No single dominant LinkedIn group for CU compliance was identified. The community is fragmented across:
  - America's Credit Unions LinkedIn page (organizational, not a group)
  - Individual state league LinkedIn pages
  - CUES LinkedIn page
  - Individual compliance officer profiles
- **Action:** Target individual compliance officers via LinkedIn outreach using the CUCO (Certified Credit Union Compliance Officer) designation as a search filter. Also search for "credit union compliance" in LinkedIn Groups and join any with 500+ members.

## Newsletters

### NAFCU Compliance Newsletters & Blog
- **URL:** https://www.nafcu.org/compliance-newsletters-blog
- **Type:** Industry newsletter (now part of America's Credit Unions post-merger)
- **Audience:** NAFCU member credit unions (historically ~70% of federal CU assets)
- **Access:** Subscription. Guest article pitches possible through their compliance blog.
- **Action:** Pitch a guest article on compliance documentation automation. NAFCU's compliance content is heavily consumed.

### The Compliance Corner Newsletter (The League)
- **URL:** https://the-league.coop/compliance-solutions
- **Type:** Bi-weekly newsletter
- **Audience:** Compliance staff at League member credit unions (Midwest)
- **Access:** Subscription through League membership.
- **Action:** Pitch a guest contribution or sponsor a newsletter issue.

### CU Times Newsletters
- **URL:** https://www.cutimes.com/newsletters
- **Type:** Industry trade publication newsletters
- **Audience:** CU Times reaches "ALL credit union decision makers" per their advertising page. Published since 1990; paper of record for the CU industry.
- **Access:** Free email newsletters available. Advertising and sponsored content options at https://www.cutimes.com/advertise-with-us.
- **Action:** Subscribe to monitor industry conversations. When ready, purchase sponsored content or native advertising placement.

### CUInsight Daily
- **URL:** https://www.cuinsight.com
- **Type:** Digital trade publication + newsletter
- **Audience:** Credit union leaders. Leading digital source for CU community news.
- **Access:** Free press release submission. Podcast and virtual event sponsorship available.
- **Action:** Submit press releases for product launches. Pitch a guest article. CUInsight accepts contributed content from industry participants.

### NCUA Email Alerts
- **URL:** https://ncua.gov/subscribe
- **Type:** Regulatory alerts from the federal regulator
- **Audience:** Every credit union that wants to stay current on NCUA guidance
- **Access:** Free subscription. Not a channel for distribution, but critical for monitoring regulatory changes that create demand for Comply.
- **Action:** Subscribe. Use new regulations as hooks for outreach ("New NCUA guidance on X just dropped. Here's how Comply helps you document compliance").

### Aux Compliance Calendar/Newsletter
- **URL:** https://auxteam.com/resources/compliance-calendar-newsletter-archive
- **Type:** Compliance calendar + newsletter
- **Audience:** Credit unions using Aux compliance services
- **Access:** Newsletter archive is public.
- **Action:** Monitor their content for compliance trends. Pitch partnership or guest content.

### Sheshunoff Credit Union Regulatory Compliance Report
- **URL:** Available via LexisNexis (https://store.lexisnexis.com)
- **Type:** Monthly newsletter
- **Audience:** Credit union compliance professionals
- **Access:** Paid subscription.
- **Action:** Lower priority. Good for understanding what compliance officers are reading, but harder to place content.

## Outreach Priority

Ranked by expected ROI (considering audience relevance, access difficulty, and cost).

### Tier 1: Highest ROI (Do First)

1. **NCUA CUSO Registry cold outreach** -- Free. Download compliance-tagged CUSOs, email them a partnership pitch. These are organizations that already sell compliance services and need better tooling.

2. **America's Credit Unions Compliance Community** -- Join as a vendor member. Contribute useful content. This is where compliance officers actually talk to each other.

3. **CUInsight guest article + press release** -- Free or low cost. Write a piece on "Why compliance documentation is still manual at most credit unions." Reaches the entire CU leadership audience.

4. **InfoSight360 integration pitch** -- If Comply can plug into or complement InfoSight360, that is instant distribution to all 50 state leagues. One partnership, thousands of CUs.

5. **Comply-YES! partnership** -- Direct outreach to a CUSO that does exactly what Comply automates. They either become a customer, a reseller, or a competitor. Find out which.

### Tier 2: High ROI (Do Within 60 Days)

6. **Compliance & Risk Council Conference (Sep 2026)** -- Apply to sponsor or present. This is the annual gathering of the exact buyer persona.

7. **CU Times sponsored content** -- Paid, but reaches "ALL credit union decision makers." Time it with a product launch or a regulatory change.

8. **NACUSO membership + conference** -- Gets you in front of every CUSO. Slower to build relationships but the partnerships are the distribution.

9. **Curql Accelerate application** -- If Comply is serious about credit union distribution at scale, becoming a CUSO through Curql is the most structured path. $100K investment + 12 weeks of introductions.

10. **Top 5 state league compliance departments** -- California, Texas, New York, Ohio, Michigan. Pitch directly to their compliance teams. Offer a free pilot for their member CUs.

### Tier 3: Supplementary (Ongoing)

- NAFCU/America's CUs compliance blog guest articles
- Compliance Decoded podcast guest pitch
- CU 2.0 podcast guest pitch
- LinkedIn outreach to CUCO-certified professionals
- CU*Answers Conversations on Compliance event
- Reddit monitoring (passive only)
- NCUA email alerts (for demand-trigger monitoring)
