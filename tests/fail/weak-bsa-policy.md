# BSA Policy

We try to comply with the Bank Secrecy Act as soon as possible when resources permit.

Our BSA program is under development and we plan to implement all required components in a timely manner.

## Reporting

We will file reports per applicable regulations when we become aware of suspicious activity. Our staff should report anything unusual to management periodically.

## Training

Training will be provided as needed, budget permitting. We intend to provide annual training if staffing allows.

## Customer Info

We collect customer info including SSN and DOB per federal regulations. PII is handled per our privacy policy.
