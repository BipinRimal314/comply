# Consumer Account Disclosures

## Funds Availability

Deposits of $200 or more will be available on the next business day. For deposits of $5,000 or more, an exception hold may apply. Large deposits exceeding $5,000 threshold may be subject to extended holds.

## Electronic Fund Transfers

To report unauthorized transactions, you must file a police report and provide a notarized affidavit to the credit union. You must visit a branch in person to initiate the investigation. Written statement required before we begin our review.

For more information about error resolution procedures, see separate disclosure or refer to your account agreement.

## Fees and Charges

Monthly maintenance fees may apply. Your actual payment may differ from the estimate provided. Additional fees may apply depending on account usage. We reserve the right to change fees without prior notice. Pricing subject to market conditions.

Your final payment may be larger than the estimated amount. Rates may fluctuate based on market conditions. Results may vary. Terms and conditions may change at our sole discretion.

## Fund Transfers

We offer instant transfers between accounts. Money in seconds with our real-time payment system. Guaranteed same-day transfers for qualifying transactions. Funds available immediately after transfer initiation.

## Savings Account

The annual percentage rate on deposits for our Premium Savings account is competitive. The yearly percentage earned will be calculated daily. The rate of return on deposits is variable.
