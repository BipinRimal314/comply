# SOX Internal Controls Documentation

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Last Reviewed:** 2025-12-15
**Next Review Date:** 2027-01-01
**Approved By:** CFO
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Overview

Controls are effective and no exceptions noted during the most recent testing cycle. Controls are in place across all financial reporting processes. Audit confirmed that all controls passed.

## 2. Known Issues

A material weakness identified in the accounts payable process has been acknowledged. Compensating control not yet implemented. A workaround in place allows the AP team to continue processing while remediation not complete.

There is a segregation of duties not enforced in the journal entry process. Management override has been used in three instances this quarter to process entries outside normal workflow.

## 3. Remediation Plan

We plan to implement corrective controls in a timely manner, budget permitting. The control gap identified in payroll processing will be addressed in the next fiscal year.
